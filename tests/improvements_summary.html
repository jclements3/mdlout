mdlout v0.2 progress at a glance

Snapshot of this session's progress (2026-05-22 to 2026-05-23). The SVG back end (z53.c) went from "renders pages" to a measured user-guide pipeline: mean structural similarity vs the PostScript reference climbed from the ~0.85 era to 0.9510 on the 200 dpi row, while the warm-cache wall time on the User's Guide dropped from 7 minutes to under 23 seconds. The snippet corpus grew from 53 to 90 entries, the cookbook from 10 to 44 recipes, and nine tagged releases (v0.2.0 - v0.2.8) shipped in two days.
